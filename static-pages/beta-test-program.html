Your Questions, Answered Transparently

Do I need to be a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit to apply?
No! We welcome all types of fundraisers including registered nonprofits, community organizers, grassroots movements, individual fundraisers, and mission-driven projects. Our transparency tools work for any fundraising effort where donors want to see how their money is used.
Is this really free? What's the catch?
Yes, it is 100% free. There are no platform fees during the beta program. The "catch" is that we need your honest, constructive feedback. Your insights are the most valuable asset as we refine the platform for fundraisers of all sizes.
How is the $100 winner selected?
The winner is selected via a random drawing from the pool of all beta testers who met the eligibility requirements for that month (active usage, completed survey, processed donations).
What happens after the beta program ends?
You will have the opportunity to continue using the platform as a "Founding Member," which will include exclusive benefits and preferential pricing. There is no obligation to continue.
How much of a time commitment is this really?
The required commitment is minimal: about 15 minutes per month to complete the feedback survey. We also offer optional 30-minute calls if you're interested in providing deeper insights.
